Covid Almanac is a list of resources for researchers and people looking for ways they can help during this global pandemic. If you are feeling unwell contact your doctor. If you are looking for official information try the CDC.

About

Hi, I'm Dan! I am currently maintaining COVID Almanac, which is just a reference of resources. The Almanac offers a signal of credibility using Twitter endorsements which appears next to the resource link. The starring feature uses your browser's local storage to keep track of what you think is important (by definition this stays on your device).

If you want to know when the site has been updated, click the Twitter logo and follow @CovidAlmanac.

If you want to contribute, send a Tweet with the resource, category, and Twitter endorsements. Please try to make sure the resource is relevant to the situation. For example, ZoomRoulette.heroku.com is on the side of relevance, but some random app or game that happens to be fun in isolation may not be. Endorsements on Twitter are important for helping decide relevance.
